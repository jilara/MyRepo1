What is Dead By Daylight

Dead By Daylight is a asymmetrical multiplayer game with a maximum of four palyer working together to survive and escape a singular killer who goal is to sacrifice the survivors to the entity. Each survivor and killer has there own stories usually connected a horror film or legend. The maps that come with the game are also based of a horror film or an area related to the killer and can give advantage to the killer or survivor. This game comes with some really thrilling chases and jump scares that will get your heart racing. This game is not recommened to the feint of heart for the gory scenes shown throughout the game.

Who and When?

The developers and publishers are the Behavior Interactive group as of january 1, 2018 after gaining publishing rights from Starbreeze Sudios and its release was in June 14, 2016.
