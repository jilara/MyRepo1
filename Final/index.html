Who are these artist?

Michelangelo

Michelangelo di Lodovico Buonarroti Simoni simply know as Michelangelo. He is widely known as an artist for painting on the Sistine Chapel but he also is an architect, sculptor, and a poet. He was born in March 6, 1475 at the Caprese, Republic of Florence and passed away in February 18, 1564

Raphael

Raffaello Sanzio or Raffaello Santi both his name in Italian. Born in April 6, 1483 at Urbino, Duchy of Urbino Italy and died in april 6, 1520, Rome. He is know for his Madonnas and compositions in the Vatican.

Rembrandt

Rembrandt Harmenszoon van Rijn known for being a Dutch painter and a printmaker and one of the greatest story tellers in the history of art born in July 15, 1606 at Leiden Netherlands and died in October 4, 1669 Amsterdam.

Vincent Van Gogh

Vincent Willem van Gogh Dutch painter considered greatest artist after rembrandt and Post-Impressionists. His art work became extremely popular after his death and broke records in sums at auctions.
